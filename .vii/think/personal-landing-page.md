# Personal Landing Page

_Generated by /vii-office-hours on 2026-05-16._

## Problem
Vibhu has no centralized place to show potential co-founders and investors who he is, what he has built, and how he thinks — so he loses credibility and serendipitous connections.

## Target user
A like-minded founder or early-stage investor who Googles Vibhu or clicks a link he shares — today they land on his LinkedIn and get a static profile that shows no personality, no interactive demos, and no sense of his thinking.

## Success metric
3 inbound messages or expressed interest from investors or potential co-founders within 30 days of launch, measured by contact form submissions or direct DMs referencing the site.

## Non-goals
- Not a job board
- Not a games or entertainment site
- No user accounts or login of any kind

## Riskiest assumption
Highly qualified, like-minded people will be excited enough to respond and get on a call after seeing Vibhu's work — cheap test: manually reach out on LinkedIn this week sharing current projects and invite people for a coffee chat; measure reply rate, call conversion, and how many want to stay in touch.

## Prior art
LinkedIn — insufficient because it cannot showcase skills interactively, has no room for long-form thinking/blog posts, is not visually differentiating, and cannot be the target of paid ads driving to a custom branded experience.

## Constraints
- No money budget — must use free and open-source tools only
- No hard deadline
- Tech stack: Next.js 15, TypeScript — styling approach left to AI's discretion
